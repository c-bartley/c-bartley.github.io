# Hi, I'm Chris 👋

**PhD researcher** at the University of Sheffield, building speech and language technology for endangered languages.

🔬 My research focuses on making AI speech tools work with very little data — the reality for most of the world's 7,000+ languages. I work primarily with **Manx Gaelic (Gaelg)**, a Celtic language from the Isle of Man.

🌐 I created [**Gaelg AI**](https://gaelgai.im) — a free, public platform offering AI-powered text-to-speech, speech recognition, translation, and audio timestamping for Manx.

📄 First paper accepted at **Interspeech 2026**: *Bootstrapping Endangered Language ASR from Short-Form Corpora*

### What I work on

- **Low-resource ASR & TTS** — speech systems that bootstrap from hours, not thousands of hours
- **Diffusion-based voice conversion** (Grad-VC) — voice conversion without forced alignment
- **Celtic language NLP** — cross-lingual transfer across Irish, Welsh, Scottish Gaelic, Manx, Cornish, and Breton
- **Language technology infrastructure** — building the tools that let endangered languages participate in the digital world
